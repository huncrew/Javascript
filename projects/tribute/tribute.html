What made him great

He was the most prolific inventor of his time; he was entirely self-educated.
His mother introduced him to books at a young age, and encouraged him to be forever curious.
He worked tirelessy, often for 20+ hours a day, but famously said he had never worked a day in his life;
And in a way he is right, as Shrophenhaur said; pain is positive - the constant motion of a persons life
is enjoyed as he pushes himself and his will to his goals, all while enjoying the moment.
